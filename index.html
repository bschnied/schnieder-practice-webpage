    Backyard Birds of Chester, Virginia

    Chester, Virginia is home to a variety of bird species. Some of the common birds you can find in this area include[1]:

    • American Robin
    • Grey Catbird
    • Northern Cardinal
    • Brown Trasher
    • Eastern Bluebird
    • House Sparrow
    • Mourning Dove
    • Carolina Wren

    These birds can be observed in various habitats around Chester, including parks, wooded areas, and near bodies of water. Birdwatching is a popular activity in the region, and many residents enjoy spotting and identifying different bird species throughout the year.
